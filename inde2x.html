Carrots

What are carrots?

A carrot is a vegetable which is said to be the perfect health food. It has a crunchy, tasty, and highly nutritious flavour. It can taste like sweet orange-colored radish.



What animals eat carrots?

Wild rabbits and donkeys enjoy eating carrots. Some other insects like Seedcorn Maggot, Wireworms and Nematodes eat carrots as well.
